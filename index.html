Hi! I’m Jess. I’m a multimedia journalist and digital strategist. Currently I’m an editor at Refinery29, where I’ve launched the Money Diaries series, Snapchat Discover editorial team, and Digital Innovation team.
In the past, I’ve been a news editor at The Daily Meal, where I hobnobbed with chefs and started the Chefs At Home series. I’ve also written and photographed for the likes of Flavorpill, MTV Iggy (RIP), Giada Weekly, Opening Ceremony, Edible Manhattan and Brooklyn, Lonny magazine, and nymag.com. Sometimes, I’ll be a hand model, too.
